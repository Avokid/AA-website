About Turku Archipelago

The Turku archipelago is one of the world's most beautiful archipelagos, featuring thousands of islands and a unique ecosystem. This pristine environment is home to diverse wildlife, including the charming Baltic ringed seals, and offers some of the best fishing opportunities in Finland.

Our boat trips are designed to showcase the natural beauty and tranquility of this special region while maintaining the highest standards of environmental responsibility.
